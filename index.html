Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book Grace N Gather Events for a destination wedding? +
We recommend booking royal palace weddings in Rajasthan (Udaipur, Jaipur, Jodhpur) or Goa 6 to 12 months in advance to secure prime venues and artist availability.
Do you handle full end-to-end multi-day Indian wedding events? +
Yes! We specialize in managing all wedding functions including Haldi, Mehendi, Cocktail, Sangeet night, Baraat procession, Pheras, and Grand Reception.
Can you customize traditional Indian Mandaps and modern theme decor? +
Absolutely. Every design, floral Mandap dome, stage layout, and lighting scheme is 100% custom-crafted to match your family aesthetics and cultural preferences.
Do you manage corporate summits and artist bookings in India? +
Yes, we regularly produce corporate galas in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, and Bengaluru, along with managing top Bollywood singers, Sufi bands, and celebrity hosts.
