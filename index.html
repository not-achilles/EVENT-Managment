Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book our event with AURA & GOLD? +
We recommend booking destination weddings and major galas 6 to 12 months in advance. However, our team is equipped to execute bespoke private celebrations with shorter turnarounds of 4 to 8 weeks.
Do you handle international destination events? +
Yes! We specialize in destination weddings and international brand launches across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the Americas with full travel and logistics support.
Can you customize decor and themes to our specific vision? +
Absolutely. Every design, floral installation, stage layout, and lighting scheme is 100% custom-created from scratch to reflect your preferences.
What is included in your full-service event management? +
Full-service management includes venue procurement, theme conceptualization, vendor coordination, staging & production, catering logistics, guest hospitality, artist management, and on-site event execution.
